# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Please be respectful, constructive, and generous in review and discussion.

Examples of good community behavior:

- asking questions in good faith
- giving actionable feedback
- disagreeing without hostility
- keeping discussions focused on the work

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, and deliberately disruptive conduct.

## Scope

This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, and other repository spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when needed to protect the health of the project.
